The Many Breeds of Pesto What is he today?

A Little Background

Pesto is a very sweet and extremely busy 10 month old lab-mix. The rescue thought he'd get to be about 35 lbs and guessed that he's probably a lab-pitty. As he gets older, his breed is anyone's guess. Over the last month, parts of his coat seem to be turning chocolate and at his last check up he was about 55 lbs.
